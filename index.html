Our Products

Feric/Non-Feric Alum

The Ferric / Non-Ferric Alum (Aluminium Sulphate) offered by us is used mainly in the pulp and paper industry and as a flocculating agent or coagulant in water and waste treatment for clarifying and or purifying water and sewage for clarifying fats, oils and petroleum products as a leather tanning agent as a pH stabilizer.

Magnesium Sulphate

Magnesium sulphate is used as micro nutrient in special application, particularly in Magnesium deficient soils and for plantation such as coffee, tea, etc. Considered as ideal nutrient to provide the Magnesium requirement of crops which supplements Sulphur simultaneously. It is also used in textile industry for dyeing, in tanning of leather and hides.

Zinc Sulphate

Zinc fertilizer is a simple, sustainable solution to zinc deficiency in soils, crops, and humans, which ultimately leads to increased food and nutrition security and higher income for farmers.We are engaged in importing and supplying a wide range of Zinc Sulphate that is widely used in various industries. These are supplied in various parts of the country in customized packaging.

Manganese Sulphate

Manganese, supplied as manganese sulphate, is used to alleviate Mn deficiencies.It is sold under different trade names as an agricultural fungicide and is widely used for controlling crop and cereal diseases.

Copper Sulphate

It is used in organic synthesis, as an herbicide, fungicide, pesticide. Mixed with lime it is called bordeaux mixture to control fungus on grapes and other berries.

Ferrous Sulphate

The coagulant of choice for many industrial and sanitary wastewater treatment applications, due to its high efficiency, effectiveness in clarification, and utility as a sludge dewatering agent.

Bauxite Powder

Bauxite(Aluminium Hydroxide)is one of the most important industrial minerals and it being the aluminium ore, is the principal raw material of alumina. Bauxite is also used as a raw material for many other products like Alumina, Cement, Abrasives, High strength proppant, Emery, Alum etc.

Magnesium Oxide

Magnesium oxide powder is used mainly as magnesium supplements in farm feeds and fertilizers. Magnesium oxide is a versatile product with many other uses including applications in the pulp and paper Industry, water treatment, refractories, tanning, sorrel cement manufacture and many other uses

Raw Magnesite Powder

This product is processed from the finest mineral deposits of Magnesite in Salem,Tamilnadu under the supervision of Experienced Personel. It can be supplied in various sizes.
